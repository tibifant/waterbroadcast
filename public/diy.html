DIY TUTORIAL
1

Materials Needed

  • 2x ESP32 microcontrollers
  • 2x Soil moisture sensors
  • 2x Water pumps with tubing
  • 2x Water reservoirs
  • 2x Power supplies (5V)
  • Jumper wires and breadboards
  • Plant pots with plants
2

Hardware Setup

  1. Connect the soil moisture sensor to your ESP32 (A0 pin)
  2. Connect the water pump to a transistor and power supply
  3. Connect the transistor gate to a GPIO pin on the ESP32
  4. Repeat this setup for both units
3

Software Installation

Clone our GitHub repository and upload the code to both ESP32s:

git clone https://github.com/water-broadcast/iot-project.git
cd iot-project
platformio run --target upload

Configure the WiFi credentials in the config.h file

4

Pairing Devices

  1. Power on both devices
  2. Press the pairing button on both units simultaneously
  3. Wait for the LED to turn solid green (indicates successful pairing)
  4. Test the connection by watering one plant - the other should activate simultaneously
5

Final Installation

  • Place the soil moisture sensor in your plant's soil
  • Mount the water pump in your water reservoir
  • Route the tubing to your plant
  • Secure all components in an enclosure
  • Enjoy your connected watering system!
